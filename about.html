About Me

My Story

My name is Jennifer Schroeder. I am an artist and a designer out of Austin, TX. My background is in fine arts with a BFA in studio art and an art education certification. I taught art for 17 years and have been a practicing artist for my entire adulthood. These experiences have given me a deep understanding of visual design and communication.

In addition to my BFA, I have completed the Career Foundry UI Design course which has enabled me to transfer my well-built visual design skills to app development.

Aside from creating art, I love being in nature. I enjoy camping, swimming, and hiking and hanging out with the best person on earth, my teenage daughter.
